Sara Van Hoezen

BIO

Drummer and singer Isolde Lasoen is no longer known only as 'drummer of DAAN'. Over the years she has proven to be a versatile musician who commits herself to various bands and projects with great passion and dedication.

After years of being a side woman, Isolde felt more and more like creating her own band, singing and writing music. She started her very own band in 2012, released her first EP L'Inconnu in 2014 (Coucou Records), and a few years later she composed and released her first full album "Cartes Postales" (2017).

In her solo project, simply called ISOLDE LASOEN, she combines drums and lead vocals and has been accompanied by Les Bens since its inception. Together with her excellent musicians, Isolde built up a strong live reputation and performed a long series of successful concerts in various formations.

Nowadays, Isolde's career takes a new turn with a brand new and ambitious solo album. "This is 100% what I want, this record is 100% ME", she says about 'OH DEAR', which is out on 8 March 2023.
